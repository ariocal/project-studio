LET'S START YOUR NEW PROJECT

I love the mutual collaboration between professional-client, I prefer the phrase:

I work with you

Instead of: I work for you

At Pixel Dev, close interaction with customers is deeply valued. We work to establish a collaborative environment where your ideas and opinions are essential. This dynamic not only guarantees the creation of customized websites, but also encourages a process in which clients feel an essential part of the project.

Transparency and open communication are priorities, ensuring that every step of website development aligns with your expectations. We strive to offer a service that not only focuses on technical quality, but also adapts to the specific needs and business goals of clients, thus creating a strong and successful partnership on each project.

Step 1

Budget Consultation and Estimation

We start with a meeting to understand your goals and needs for the website. During this stage, we discuss the scope of the project and provide a detailed budget estimate. This step is essential to align with your expectations and plan the next steps of website development.

Step 2

Design and Structure

Once the objectives are established, we begin to work on the structure and design of the website. We will create wireframes or initial sketches that represent the distribution of content and navigation of the site. Your feedback is vital at this stage to ensure the design meets your expectations.

Step 3

Development and Construction

With the design approved, we moved towards the development of the website. Here visual and functional elements are integrated, pages are created, functionalities are implemented, and the design is adjusted as development progresses. During this process, we will keep you informed and you will be able to make revisions.

Step 4

Review and Tests

Once the website build is complete, we perform extensive testing to make sure everything is working properly. We'll give you access to review the site and test it yourself. Any necessary adjustments or corrections are made at this stage.

Step 5

Delivery and Maintenance

After your final approval, we deliver the website and provide you with the necessary training to manage it. Additionally, we offer maintenance services to ensure your site is working properly and is up to date. We are available to provide ongoing support and make adjustments based on your changing needs.*

* Any additional changes will generate extra rates charged per hour. Hourly rate: $35 usd +taxes.
